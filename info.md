# Home Energy Manager

Home Assistant integration for solar, battery, grid, costing, and forecasting
workflows.

## Focus

- solar and battery monitoring
- scheduling and control
- cost-aware automations
- battery wear cost tracking
- forecasting and planning
- staged settings with submit/discard behavior

## Notes

The current codebase begins with ByteWatt / Neovolt backend support, but the
project is intentionally broader and meant to support additional energy
systems over time.
